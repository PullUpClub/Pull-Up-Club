Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookie Banner

When you first visit Pull-Up Club, you'll see a cookie banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies for the full experience
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences by cookie type
  • Learn more about each cookie category

Browser Settings

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Third-Party Opt-Outs

You can opt out of third-party tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Meta Pixel: Facebook Ad Preferences

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Essential Cookies

Disabling essential cookies will significantly impact your experience:

  • You'll need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some features may not work properly
  • Competition submissions may fail

Non-Essential Cookies

Disabling non-essential cookies will:

  • Reduce personalization of your experience
  • Limit our ability to improve the platform
  • Show less relevant advertisements
  • May result in slower performance

Mobile Apps

Our mobile apps may use similar tracking technologies. You can manage these through:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Privacy > Ads
  • App Settings: Privacy preferences within the Pull-Up Club app

Updates to This Policy
