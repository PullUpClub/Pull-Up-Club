Badge System

Athletes progress through five badge levels based on their performance:

Elite

The highest honor - reserved for exceptional athletes with consistent top-tier performance.

Hardened

Experienced competitors who have proven their strength and dedication over multiple competitions.

Operator

Skilled athletes demonstrating advanced pull-up capabilities and technique.

Proven

Reliable performers who have shown consistent improvement and solid results.

Recruit

New members beginning their journey in the Pull-Up Club community.

Leaderboard Features

  • Monthly Competitions - Fresh challenges every month with new opportunities to climb

  • Global Scope - Compete against athletes from around the world
  • Verified Results - All submissions professionally reviewed for accuracy
