üìπ Video Submission Standards

Camera Setup Requirements:

  • Full body must be visible throughout the entire performance
  • Camera should be positioned at least 6 feet away
  • Clear view of starting and ending positions
  • Adequate lighting to see form and movement clearly
  • Stable camera position (no shaking or movement)

Technical Requirements:

  • Format: MP4, MOV, or AVI
  • Duration: Maximum 5 minutes
  • Quality: Minimum 720p resolution
  • Size: Maximum 100MB file size
  • Audio: Not required but recommended for counting

üí™ Proper Pull-Up Form

Starting Position:

  • Dead hang with arms fully extended
  • Overhand grip (palms facing away)
  • Hands shoulder-width apart or slightly wider
  • Feet must not touch the ground or any support
  • Body should be relatively still before beginning

Upward Movement:

  • Pull body up using arms and back muscles
  • Chin must clearly pass above the bar
  • No kipping, swinging, or momentum assistance
  • Controlled, smooth movement required
  • No assistance from legs or jumping

Downward Movement:

  • Controlled descent to full dead hang
  • Arms must reach full extension before next rep
  • No dropping or rapid descent
  • Brief pause in dead hang position between reps

‚öñÔ∏è Judging Criteria

Valid Repetitions:

  • Full range of motion (dead hang to chin over bar)
  • Proper form maintained throughout
  • No assistance or momentum
  • Clear pause between repetitions
  • Controlled movement both up and down

Invalid Repetitions:

  • Incomplete range of motion
  • Kipping, swinging, or momentum assistance
  • Leg assistance or touching ground
  • Not reaching chin over bar
  • Not returning to full dead hang
  • Excessive swinging or body movement
