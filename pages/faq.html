Everything you need to know about joining and beginning your Pull-Up Club journey.

What is Pull-Up Club?
Pull-Up Club is a monthly fitness competition platform focused on pull-up challenges. Members compete globally, earn badges based on performance, and climb leaderboards while being part of a supportive fitness community.
How much does membership cost?
Membership is $9.99 per month, which gives you access to monthly competitions, leaderboard rankings, badge progression, and our full community platform. You can cancel anytime.
Do I need to be able to do pull-ups already?
No! We welcome athletes of all skill levels. Our badge system starts with "Recruit" for beginners, and we provide training resources to help you improve. The goal is progress, not perfection.
What equipment do I need?
You need access to a pull-up bar and a device to record video (smartphone is fine). The bar can be at a gym, in your home, or at a public fitness area‚Äîas long as it allows for proper pull-up form.

üèÜ Competitions & Rules

Learn about our monthly competitions, judging standards, and competition format.

How often are competitions held?
We hold one competition each month. New challenges are announced on the 1st, submissions are accepted from the 20th-30th, and results are published at the beginning of the following month.
What makes a valid pull-up?
Valid pull-ups must start from a dead hang (arms fully extended), pull until chin clears the bar, and return to full dead hang. No kipping, swinging, or momentum assistance is allowed. Check our Rules page for complete details.
How are submissions judged?
All video submissions are reviewed by trained judges who verify proper form, count valid repetitions, and ensure competition standards are met. You'll receive feedback if your submission doesn't meet requirements.
Can I resubmit if my video is rejected?
Yes, if your submission is rejected for technical or form issues, you can resubmit before the monthly deadline. We provide specific feedback to help you improve your next attempt.

üìπ Video Submissions

Technical requirements and best practices for video submissions.

What are the video requirements?
Videos must be clear, show your full body throughout the performance, and be recorded from at least 6 feet away. Format should be MP4, MOV, or AVI, maximum 100MB file size, minimum 720p resolution.
Can someone else record my video?
Absolutely! Having someone else record often provides better camera angles and stability. Just ensure they follow our video guidelines for optimal submission quality.
What if my video file is too large?
Try compressing your video or reducing the recording quality slightly. Most smartphones allow you to adjust video quality in camera settings. The maximum file size is 100MB.

How do I earn higher badges?

My video upload is failing. What should I do?
First, check that your file meets our requirements (under 100MB, MP4/MOV/AVI format). Try a stable internet connection and ensure your browser is up to date. If problems persist, contact support.
I can't access my account. What now?
Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you're still having trouble, contact our support team with your registered email address.

Log into your account and go to the Profile section. You can update your personal information, contact details, and competition preferences there.
